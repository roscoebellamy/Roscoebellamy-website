On The Court

TOP 6
IN THE WORLD

A Los Angeles native, Roscoe was the No. 1 junior tennis player in the country before competing at UCLA. Since transitioning to pickleball in 2023, he has quickly risen to the top of the game — winning his first PPA Gold Medal at the end of 2025 and solidifying himself as a top six player in the world.

Lifestyle

BEYOND
THE GAME

Roscoe is as active off the court as he is on it. From competing on ESPN's Pickleball Slam alongside Andre Agassi and Andy Roddick, to playing at Necker Island and exclusive celebrity pickleball events around the world — he's become one of the key figures helping bridge the gap between professional pickleball and mainstream celebrity culture.

The Pickleballers Podcast

25M+
VIEWS

Co-hosted with The Kitchen Pickleball and picked up by PickleballTV after two seasons, the Pickleballers podcast is the most-watched pickleball show in the world. Guests have included Andre Agassi, Drew Brees, Ben Johns, Terrell Owens, Anna Leigh Waters, and John Mackey — with new episodes reaching audiences across YouTube, Amazon Prime, and PickleballTV.

The Pickleball Collective

100+
MEMBERS

Roscoe's mission is bigger than his own results. He founded The Pickleball Collective — an instructional community with over 100 members where he has created curriculum and systems to help players improve, learn more about the sport, and come together as a community. It's the direct line between a Top 6 pro and the players who want to reach the next level.
